JetBrains Memory Game

Game Rules

  1. 2 flips are considered as 1 move

  2. Attempt to complete both the levels (level 1 and level 2) in the least time and moves

  3. The supported browsers are Chrome (version: 73 and above) and Safari (version: 12 and above); in mobile, only Portrait mode is supported.

You will get a chance to win the goodies only if you complete both levels & submit the score. Each of the top 10 winners will receive a 1-year JetBrains license for an IDE. Each of the next 10 winners (ranks 11th - 20th) will get a JetBrains T-shirt.
